# The Psychology of everyday Actions
#Books/The_Design_of_Everyday_Things #Design

To identify an action we make use of signifiers, constraints, mappings, and a conceptual model. We evaluate the action through the use of feedback and a comparison with our conceptual model. If we get an error we modify our conceptual model and actions until we understand how to use the device.
