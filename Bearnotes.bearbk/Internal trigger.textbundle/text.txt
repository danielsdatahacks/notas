# Internal trigger
#Books/Hooked #Design #Psychology

Thoughts / feelings that appear automatically in your mind. Example: Feelings of boredom, loneliness, frustration, confusion, and indecisiveness often lead to a slight feeling of pain and consequently to some action that is taken to relief this pain.
Instagrams trigger: A person fears a special moment will be lost forever —> Uses instagram to save it.

If software solves these pains regularly a habit will be formed, i.e. users will use this product when experiencing certain internal triggers. —> Learned association of trigger and action.
