# Conscious vs. Subconscious Systems of Cognition
#Books/The_Design_of_Everyday_Things #Design #Psychology

Subconscious: Fast, Automatic, Multiple resources, controls skilled behavior

Conscious: Slow, controlled, limited resources, invoked for novel situations: when learning, when in danger, when things go wrong.
